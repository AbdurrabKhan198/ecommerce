Returns & Exchange Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction with easy returns and exchanges

Return Policy

Return Window
  • 7 days from delivery date
  • Items must be unused
  • Original tags attached
  • Resalable condition
What Can Be Returned
  • Wrong size
  • Defective items
  • Not as described
  • Damaged in transit

How to Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and reason for return.

  • Email: Kingdupattahouse@gmail.com
  • Phone: +91 7860247786
  • WhatsApp: +91 7499099900
Step 2: Get Return Authorization

We'll send you a return authorization number and instructions.

Step 3: Package Securely

Package the item securely with original tags and include the return authorization number.

Step 4: Send Back

Send the package back to us using the provided return address.

Step 5: Receive Refund

We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days after receiving the item.

Exchange Policy

Size Exchange
  • Free size exchange within 7 days
  • Item must be unused with tags
  • We'll arrange free pickup
  • Send correct size if available
Exchange Process
  1. Contact us for exchange
  2. We'll verify size availability
  3. Arrange pickup of original item
  4. Send replacement item

Refund Policy

Refund Timeline
  • Processing: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • UPI/Cards: 1-3 business days
  • Total Time: 7-14 business days
Refund Methods
  • Original payment method
  • Bank transfer
  • UPI refund
  • Store credit (optional)
Note: Refund will be processed to the original payment method. Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error.

Non-Returnable Items

Items We Cannot Accept
  • Used or worn items
  • Items without original tags
  • Damaged by customer
  • Personalized items
  • Sale/clearance items
Special Cases
  • Underwear (hygiene reasons)
  • Items damaged by misuse
  • Items returned after 7 days
  • Items not in original packaging
