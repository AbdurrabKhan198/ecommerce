Care Guide

How to care for your King Dupatta House garments to keep them looking beautiful

Dupattas Care

Washing Instructions
  • Hand Wash: Preferred method for delicate fabrics
  • Cold Water: Use cold or lukewarm water
  • Mild Detergent: Use gentle, color-safe detergent
  • No Bleach: Never use bleach or harsh chemicals
Drying & Storage
  • Air Dry: Hang to dry in shade
  • No Direct Sun: Avoid direct sunlight
  • Iron on Low: Use low heat setting
  • Fold Carefully: Store folded to prevent creases

Leggings Care

Washing Instructions
  • Machine Wash: Use gentle cycle
  • Cold Water: Cold water only
  • Mild Detergent: Use gentle detergent
  • Separate Colors: Wash with similar colors
Drying & Storage
  • Air Dry: Hang to dry or lay flat
  • No Dryer: Avoid machine drying
  • No Iron: Do not iron
  • Fold Neatly: Store folded in drawer

Pants Care

Washing Instructions
  • Machine Wash: Use normal cycle
  • Cold Water: Cold or warm water
  • Regular Detergent: Standard detergent is fine
  • Turn Inside Out: Wash inside out
Drying & Storage
  • Air Dry: Hang to dry
  • Iron if Needed: Use medium heat
  • Hang Storage: Store on hangers
  • Fold Alternative: Can be folded if needed

General Care Tips

Before First Wear
  • Check care labels
  • Wash before first wear
  • Check for loose threads
  • Try on for fit
Stain Removal
  • Treat stains immediately
  • Use gentle stain removers
  • Test on hidden area first
  • Blot, don't rub

Fabric-Specific Care

Cotton
  • Machine wash cold
  • Air dry preferred
  • Iron on medium heat
  • May shrink slightly
Polyester
  • Machine wash cold
  • Quick dry
  • Iron on low heat
  • Wrinkle resistant
Blended Fabrics
  • Follow care label
  • Use gentle cycle
  • Air dry recommended
  • Iron carefully

Storage Tips

Dupattas Storage
  • Fold carefully to prevent creases
  • Store in dry place
  • Use mothballs if needed
  • Rotate regularly
Leggings & Pants Storage
  • Hang or fold neatly
  • Keep in dry place
  • Separate by color
  • Use drawer dividers
