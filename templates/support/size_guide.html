How to Measure

For Dupattas:
  • Measure your height
  • Consider your frame size
  • Think about draping style preference
For Leggings & Pants:
  • Measure waist at narrowest point
  • Measure hips at fullest point
  • Measure inseam for length
Pro Tips
  • When in doubt, size up for comfort
  • Consider your body shape, not just measurements
  • Check our return policy if you need to exchange
  • Contact us for personalized sizing advice
