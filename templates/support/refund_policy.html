Refund Policy

Our commitment to your satisfaction with our refund process

Refund Policy Overview

Refund Eligibility
  • 7-day return window
  • Unused items with tags
  • Defective products
  • Wrong items received
Refund Timeline
  • Processing: 5-7 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • UPI/Cards: 1-3 business days
  • Total: 7-14 business days

Refund Process

Step 1: Request Refund

Contact us within 7 days of delivery with your order number and reason for refund.

Step 2: Return Authorization

We'll provide return authorization and instructions for sending the item back.

Step 3: Return Item

Package the item securely with original tags and send it back to us.

Step 4: Quality Check

We'll inspect the returned item to ensure it meets our return criteria.

Step 5: Process Refund

Once approved, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Methods

Original Payment Method
  • Credit/Debit Cards
  • Net Banking
  • UPI Payments
  • Digital Wallets
Alternative Methods
  • Bank Transfer
  • Store Credit
  • Cash Refund (for COD orders)
  • Exchange for other products

Refund Conditions

Eligible for Refund
  • Defective items
  • Wrong items received
  • Items not as described
  • Damaged in transit
  • Size issues (if exchange not possible)
Not Eligible for Refund
  • Used or worn items
  • Items without original tags
  • Damaged by customer
  • Personalized items
  • Sale/clearance items

Partial Refunds

When Partial Refunds Apply
  • Minor defects: 10-20% discount on the item
  • Minor damage: 15-25% discount on the item
  • Color variations: 5-10% discount on the item
  • Size issues: Exchange preferred over refund
Note: Partial refunds are offered as store credit or can be applied to future purchases.
