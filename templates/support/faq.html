Ordering & Payment

Simply browse our products, select your desired items, choose your size, and add them to your cart. Proceed to checkout, enter your details, and complete the payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with your order details.

We accept all major payment methods including:
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Net Banking
  • UPI Payments
  • Digital Wallets (Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) for select areas

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 7-day return policy for unused items in original condition with tags attached. Items must be in resalable condition. Return shipping is free for defective items, otherwise, return shipping charges apply.

  1. Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll send you a return authorization
  4. Package the item securely with original tags
  5. Send it back to us
  6. We'll process your refund within 5-7 business days

Size & Fit

Use our detailed size guide available on each product page. Measure yourself and compare with our size charts. When in doubt, size up for comfort. You can also contact us for personalized sizing advice.

If the size doesn't fit, you can exchange it for a different size within 7 days of delivery. We'll arrange a free pickup and send you the correct size. If the desired size is not available, we'll provide a full refund.
