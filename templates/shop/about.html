Our Story

King Dupatta House has been serving customers since 1982, establishing itself as a trusted name in traditional and modern women's wear. Located in the heart of Lucknow at Akbari Gate, we have built a reputation for quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction over the past four decades.

From our physical store in Lucknow's historic Nakkhas area, we have expanded to serve customers nationwide through our online platform. We specialize in premium quality dupattas, comfortable leggings, and stylish pants and trousers that blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary fashion trends.

Our journey has been guided by a commitment to quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. We work directly with skilled artisans and manufacturers to ensure every piece meets our high standards while remaining accessible to women from all walks of life.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of satisfied customers who trust us for their daily fashion needs. From comfortable everyday wear to elegant occasion pieces, we continue to evolve and expand our collection based on what our customers love most.
